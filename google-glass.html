MEDITATE - A MEDITATION APP FOR GOOGLE GLASS

I wanted to create a guide for those who suffer from panic attacks to calm down. Glass is an escape window from reality, which is also a coping tactic for anxiety more than a mobile phone or tablet.

Team:

I am in the process of designing this app by myself.

Duration:

1-2 Weeks

Ways of coping with anxiety attacks:

  • Conscious breathing.
  • Escape to somewhere to feel calmness in your mind.
  • Remind yourself it will subside.
  • Remind yourself it's just a chemical imbalance.
  • Let it pass/ ride it out
  • Don't fear the attack
  • Engross yourself in something else.
  • Call a friend

    Techniques:

  • Breathe
  • Meditate
  • Reassure
  • Distract

I'm discovering that the glass is small, doable actions unlike a mobile phone with folders with apps inside them. Keeping everything on face and also easily accessible is important. In this flow, instead of breaking everything up, i combined them into a single 'meditation' with components of breathing, distraction, and reassurance.
